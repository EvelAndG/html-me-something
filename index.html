Thor: Ragnarock

This is one of the funniest marvel movies with some of the best Thor action. Director, Taika Waititi, went a completely different direction than the previous Thor movies. This was a much needed change for the series.

Certified Fresh by Rotten Tomatoes with a 92%.

Box Office: $314,971,245


Guardians of the Galaxy

This movie is a great intro to the universe of the Guardians. The Guardians are the most interesting bunch of misfits you'll find in the MCU. (Marvel Comic Universe)

Certified Fresh by Rotten Tomatoes with a 91%

Box Office: $270,592,504


Spiderman: Homecoming

In my opinion, this is the best Spider-man movie made. I'm glad it was finally done right. Tom Holland does an amazing job playing Spider-Man.

Certified Fresh by Rotten Tomatoes with a 92%

Box Office: $334,166,825


Avengers: Infinity War

Literally the most polarizing movie in all of the Marvel Franchise. People either love or hate this movie.
This has to be on every Marvel fan's top list because it includes almost all characters from the MCU.

Certified Fresh by Rotten Tomatoes with an 83%

Box Office: $664,987,816


Captain America: Civil War

After almost a decade of introducting Marvel's heroes, they are all brought together to overcome a moral impass.
This is also the first movie where Spider-man appears in the MCU

Certified Fresh by Rotten Tomatoes with a 91%

Box Office: $408,080,554
